About Us

Who are we ?

We are Teach For India fellows working in Chennai Boys Higher Secondary School, Maanthope. School has excellent infrastructure with large playgrounds, libraries, smart classrooms and laboratories. But, they have not been used regularly. There are around 70 teachers in school in addition to the HM, AHM and the three TFI teachers. Since, the school has 1700 plus students and it being a boys school has ensured that it is the biggest schools in Saidapet. All the TFI classrooms are at a year 5 intervention. There are 6 7th grade classrooms where 3 are taken up by TFI and 2 of the remaining belong to English medium and one belongs to tamil medium.

What is Teach For India ?

Teach For India (TFI) is a non profit organisation that is a part of the Teach For All network. The Fellowship recruits college graduates and working professionals to serve as full-time teachers in low-income schools for two years. Teach For India is striving to end the problem of educational inequity in India and provide an excellent education to all children.

Why are we here ?

The students are from a poor background where the parents of the most of the kids are uneducated and offer minimal support for their child's education. Kids are leading a tough life back home as many of their fathers are alcoholics leading to fewer role models they could actually look upto. Community has an adverse effect on the kids as evident from the violent behavior of the kids inside the classroom. Few students understand the importance of education for their future livelihood and are on the path for a secure adulthood. Except for the few, many are disillusioned to understand what lies ahead and their level of investment for their education needs a big push. As pointed out at the start of the passage few of the families understand the importance of the education and actually expects the kids to achieve great heights. Many other parents don't even realize the importance of their children's education as their every day chores takes dominance compared to their kids education. The school has a good infrastructure with spacious classrooms and large play grounds. Other than that the quality of education the children receive at school is way below the expectations and mandates a huge scope for improvement. Students lead a tough life back home as their exposure for negative elements overshadow the positive elements which makes them naive enough to be easy targets for picking up bad habits.
